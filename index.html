Private Prisons: The New Face of Slavery

There are a number of issues surrounding the use of private prisons by the U.S. Department of Justice (DOJ), and this website serves to give a brief outline of the histories and issues inherent to the disturbingly profitable private prison industry. These issues will be discussed under three categories: The Numbers, Profiteering, and Prison Conditions.
